# Reference baseline — pre-optimization (2026-05-30, `06166f0`)

The fixed reference point captured before backend/MLX optimization begins. Compare future
runs against this with the same fixed corpus + cell definitions (see below). Generated with
`scripts/summarize_generation_telemetry.py` from runtime telemetry; raw diagnostics are not committed.

## Conditions

- **Machine / tier:** native Apple-Silicon **8 GB** Mac → `floor_8gb_mac` (not forced). `trims`/`pressure`
  capture is live on this tier.
- **Build:** `06166f0` (Quality→Speed OOM fallback removed — Quality always loads genuine 8-bit).
- **Telemetry:** `QWENVOICE_DEBUG=1 QWENVOICE_SUPPRESS_WARMUP=1` (accurate cold for Custom/Design).
- **Matrix:** 3 modes × 2 variants (Speed 1.7B 4-bit / Quality 1.7B 8-bit). Per cell 1 cold + 3 warm,
  except Voice Cloning = 4 warm (warm-by-design). 24 takes total.
- **Quality ran genuine 8-bit** on the native floor tier — every Quality row's `modelID` is the `-8bit`
  package (peak physFoot 5.25 GB on Clone/Quality; **no OOM, no Speed fallback**).
- **Fixed corpus (reuse verbatim for comparisons):**
  - Script (all modes): `The morning train slipped quietly out of the station, carrying a handful of sleepy travelers toward the coast.`
  - Custom Voice: default speaker (Aiden – English native).
  - Voice Design: `A warm, calm middle-aged male narrator with a clear, measured pace.`
  - Voice Cloning: saved voice `A_warm_elderly_woman` (a pre-enrolled, valid-length reference;
    `Professional_male_narrator_clear` was flagged "reference too short").

## Output-quality gate

- **audioQC (objective):** 12 pass / 10 warn / 2 fail — **every** flag is `dropout:Nms` (interior
  silence). Both fails (`custom/speed/warm` 607 ms, `design/speed/cold` 653 ms) have **no chunk gaps**,
  healthy RMS (−18 to −21 dBFS), zero clicks/clipping/nonfinite.
- **Listening pass (human, mandatory gate): PASS.** The flagged silences are the **natural pause at the
  comma** ("station, … carrying"), not audio defects — confirmed by ear on the two fail clips + a pass
  clip.
- **Follow-up (not a regression):** the audioQC `dropout` threshold over-flags natural punctuation
  pauses on this corpus. Tune `makeAudioQCReport` (raise the interior-silence threshold and/or ignore
  silence at punctuation) during the optimization phase so the tripwire stops crying wolf.

## Headline observations (the optimization targets)

- **RTF < 1 across every cell** (0.49–0.78) — generation is slower than realtime on the 8 GB tier.
  Clone is slowest (0.49–0.53); Custom/Design Speed fastest (~0.74–0.78). This is the primary speed
  target.
- **Cold TTFC is dominated by model load:** Quality cold ~12–13 s, Speed cold ~3–4 s; warm TTFC ~1 s.
- **decode-loop ~9.1–13.5 s** is the bulk of wall time; Clone highest (~12.7–13.5 s).
- **Memory:** Quality 8-bit fits on 8 GB (peak physFoot 3.8–5.25 GB). `trims = 0` — no memory-pressure
  trims fired during these short generations on the native floor tier.
