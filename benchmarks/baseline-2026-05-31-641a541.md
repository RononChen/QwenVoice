# Reference baseline — pre-optimization, CLI-driven (2026-05-31, `641a541`)

The current fixed reference point for backend/MLX optimization. **First baseline measured on the
`vocello` CLI** (the now-canonical headless driver), in-process, on a native **8 GB** Mac. Supersedes
the 2026-05-30 app-driven baselines (`06166f0`, `670d5c8`) as the reference; compare future runs with
the same fixed corpus + cells, **measured the same way (CLI)**. Generated by
`scripts/summarize_generation_telemetry.py` from runtime telemetry (raw diagnostics not committed).

## Conditions

- **Machine / tier:** native Apple-Silicon **8 GB** Mac → `floor_8gb_mac` (not forced).
- **Build:** `641a541` (engine unchanged since `670d5c8` — intervening work was CLI/docs/cleanup only).
- **Driver:** `vocello bench --modes custom,design,clone --variants speed,quality --lengths short,medium,long --warm 3` (historical run; current successful runs publish through the registry automatically)
  — **in-process, no XPC**. Cold/warm controlled by explicit load/unload (no `QWENVOICE_SUPPRESS_WARMUP`).
- **Telemetry:** lightweight (bench forces `QWENVOICE_DEBUG=1`).
- **Matrix:** 3 modes × 2 variants × 3 lengths. Per cell **3 warm per length**; Custom/Design add **1
  cold** (medium) for load timing. **58 takes across 24 cells**, ~960 s. Quality ran genuine 8-bit on
  the native floor tier (no OOM, no fallback).
- **Fixed corpus (reuse verbatim):**
  - short (~35 ch): `The train left the station at dawn.`
  - medium (~110 ch): `The morning train slipped quietly out of the station, carrying a handful of sleepy travelers toward the coast.`
  - long (~344 ch): `…toward the coast. Outside the fogged windows, pale fields gave way to grey water, and the rhythm of the rails settled into a steady, hypnotic hum. By the time the sun finally broke through, most of the passengers had drifted into an unhurried silence.`
  - Custom = default speaker (Aiden); Design brief = `A warm, calm middle-aged male narrator with a clear, measured pace.`; Clone = saved voice `A_warm_elderly_woman`.

### Measurement caveats (read before comparing to the 2026-05-30 baselines)

- **Memory is in-process.** `physFoot` / `peakGPU` here are the **CLI process** footprint (UI-less
  driver + MLX + engine in one process). The shipped app runs the engine in a separate XPC service, so
  the app's Jetsam-relevant figure is **lower**. These numbers are comparable **across future CLI runs**,
  not 1:1 with the app-driven `670d5c8` baseline (which measured the isolated engine-service process).
  Hence physFoot here (e.g. custom/quality/warm/medium **5773 MB**) reads higher than `670d5c8` (4114 MB).
- **TTFC = `-`** (engine-only, non-streaming bench). For user-perceived first-chunk latency use
  `vocello generate --stream` or `vocello bench --ttfc`.
- **Clone `cold` rows are a bench artifact.** The bench unloads before every cell, so clone's first warm
  take records `warmState=cold` (the load happened inside the take). In production clone is warm-by-design
  (primed as part of the request) — there is no cold-clone path. (Minor follow-up: bench could skip the
  pre-cell unload for clone.)

## Output-quality gate

- **audioQC (objective, all 58 takes):** **40 pass / 6 warn / 12 fail.** Every flag is `dropout` (18
  total) — **no `clicks`, `nonfinite`, `clipping`, or `near_silent`**. Dropouts are concentrated in
  Custom/Design **medium + long** (comma/sentence pauses); **Clone is 100% pass**.
- **Listening pass (agy, 9 flagged cells): 8 false-positives + 1 REAL DEFECT.**
  - 8/9 flagged clips are detector false-positives — natural pauses at commas / sentence boundaries
    (e.g. after "station", between sentences), no audible glitches.
  - **1 genuine defect:** `custom/pro_custom_quality/warm/long` — an **unnatural ~586 ms mid-utterance
    silence between "fogged" and "windows"** (a non-punctuation position; the objective detector
    independently flagged the same clip `dropout:586ms`). Likely a stochastic generation artifact on long
    content rather than a code regression (engine unchanged), but it is a **real audible dropout**.
- **Verdict:** **not a clean pass** — one real dropout on long/Quality content is recorded as a
  pre-optimization quality finding (see optimization targets). The `dropout` over-flagging on natural
  pauses remains a detector-tuning item (raise the interior-silence threshold / ignore silence at
  punctuation).
- **Post-baseline update (`ac86b8a`, 2026-05-31):** the `dropout` detector-tuning item above is
  **resolved** — audioQC dropout is now punctuation-aware (it counts long pauses against the text's
  punctuation budget and flags only an *excess* beyond it or a single *egregious* ≥1200 ms gap), so
  natural sentence/comma pauses no longer false-fail. Re-running this matrix on the updated detector
  flips the false-positive `fail`/`warn` cells to `pass` (the lone residual `warn` is a real-but-natural
  ~1116 ms sentence-boundary pause). **The QC verdicts in this file are the pre-fix snapshot**, kept
  verbatim as the historical reference point; perf/memory numbers are unaffected (engine unchanged).

## Headline observations (optimization targets)

- **RTF < 1 in every cell (0.20–0.89)** — generation is slower than realtime on the 8 GB tier; the
  primary speed target. Custom/Design land ~0.74–0.89; **Clone is far slower (0.20–0.56)** and is the
  clear worst case.
- **decode-loop is the wall-time driver and scales ~linearly with length:** short ~2–9 s, medium
  ~6–14 s, long ~24–39 s. **Clone decode is the heaviest per length** (clone/quality/long ~38.6 s).
- **Memory (in-process physFoot, Jetsam-relevant-ish):** Custom ~2.4–7.3 GB · Design ~3.4–7.4 GB ·
  **Clone ~7.4–8.7 GB** (clone conditioning + base model resident GPU dominate; peakGPU hits ~9.3 GB on
  clone/quality). Grows ~2–3 GB short→long (KV cache). Mode matters more than length: **Clone ≫ Design
  ≳ Custom.** Reducing clone's resident footprint is the top memory target.
- **`trims = 0` everywhere** — no memory-pressure trims fired, even at ~8–9 GB physFoot, on the floor
  tier. (`phys_footprint` includes compressed memory, so it can exceed 8 GB physical without a kernel
  pressure warn here — worth watching during optimization.)
- **Output quality:** one **real mid-utterance dropout** (custom/quality/long, ~586 ms) is the
  standout quality finding to chase; the remaining `dropout` flags are natural-pause false-positives
  (detector tuning, not regressions).
